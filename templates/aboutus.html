About Saral

Clarity for Every Citizen

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to break down the language barriers that prevent citizens from understanding their rights and accessing essential services. We believe that information is a fundamental right, and it should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their literacy level or familiarity with complex terminology.

The Problem: The Language Wall

In India, critical information is often locked away in dense, bureaucratic language. Government schemes, legal notices, and official forms are filled with jargon that can be intimidating and confusing for the average person. This "language wall" creates a significant gap, preventing millions of people from benefiting from the very systems designed to serve them.

Our Solution: Saral

Saral (meaning "Simple") is our answer to this challenge. We leverage the power of Artificial Intelligence to act as your personal interpreter for official documents. By simply uploading a photo, our tool instantly analyzes the text, extracts the key information, and presents it in a simple, easy-to-understand summary. With multi-language support, we ensure that you can understand what's important in the language you're most comfortable with.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every citizen is empowered to make informed decisions. By democratizing information and making bureaucracy transparent, Saral aims to foster a more inclusive and knowledgeable society, one document at a time.
